How It Works

Three steps to permanent trust.

Step 01

Issue

University admins submit student data through the secure dashboard. Each credential is SHA-256 hashed and cryptographically signed.

Step 02

Store

The credential hash is minted as an immutable record on-chain. No entity can alter or delete the record once written to the ledger.

Step 03

Verify

Employers paste a Certificate ID or transaction hash. The system queries the chain and returns a tamper-proof authenticity result in seconds.
